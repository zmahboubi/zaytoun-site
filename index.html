Spa & Hammam
At Zaytoun, wellbeing was placed at the heart of the project from its very conception — and the spa is its centrepiece. Step into a traditional Moroccan hammam carved in tadelakt and warm marble, where steam, eucalyptus and black soap follow centuries-old rituals. Treatment cabins offer signature therapies built around argan oil, rose of Kelaat M'Gouna, and orange blossom. A space to slow down, breathe, and return to yourself — open to all, with priority access reserved for residents.
